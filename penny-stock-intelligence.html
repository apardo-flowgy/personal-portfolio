What the system does

From noisy market chatter to structured signals

The application is designed for research, monitoring and pattern discovery. It does not publish raw conversations, identify specific users or expose company-level source data.

01

Chat history and heatmaps

Reviews discussion history by company and date, detects weekly activity spikes and ranks periods by message volume and reaction score.

02

Price charts with context

Overlays OHLCV price behavior with message volume, unique users, insider activity, rally labels and relevant price events.

03

AI sentiment scoring

Scores bullish and bearish pressure independently, then extracts thesis, catalyst and risk-type readings for manual review.

04

Insider activity monitoring

Normalizes insider activity records and makes them comparable against discussion intensity, user behavior and price action.

05

Multi-factor screener

Filters the full universe by chat activity, insider activity, price behavior and hype metrics to create focused review lists.

06

Snapshot-based alerts

Compares the current state against previous snapshots and flags unusual combinations of volume, activity and price movement.

07

Market heat indicators

Builds aggregate indicators that estimate how hot or quiet the tracked market universe is at a given point in time.

08

Multibagger research

Identifies large historical winners and studies whether user activity, insider activity or sentiment patterns appeared before the move.

09

Signal correlations

Tests relationships between message volume, AI sentiment and later price variation over configurable time windows.

10

User activity analysis

Profiles user participation patterns, concentration, engagement quality and changes in activity over time without exposing identities publicly.

11

AI audit trail

Keeps reviewable AI outputs so sentiment classifications, highlighted readings and model behavior can be inspected manually.

12

Control panel

Provides a desktop control surface to run data updates, analysis jobs and research workflows without relying on command-line usage.

Daily research workflow

Designed as an analyst cockpit

The product is not meant to replace judgment. It compresses a large amount of discussion, insider and price activity into a shortlist that can be reviewed manually.

1Update data

Refresh discussion, price and insider-activity datasets.

2Score sentiment

Run AI classification over the latest company-day windows.

3Rank signals

Surface unusual combinations of hype, users, insiders and price action.

4Review manually

Inspect highlighted readings and decide whether a case deserves deeper work.

Status: private MVP. The next product work is improving sentiment quality, testing correlations and deciding whether the final product should be a monitoring dashboard, a research terminal or an alerting layer.
